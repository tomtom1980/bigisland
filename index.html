üí° General Tips

  • Gas: Fill up in Kona or Hilo before long drives - stations are sparse
  • Weather: Kona side is hot and dry; bring sunscreen, hats, reef-safe sunscreen
  • Driving: Allow extra time - roads are winding, speed limits low
  • Reservations: Book fine dining restaurants ahead, especially weekends
  • Beach Safety: Check conditions, respect ocean warnings, don't turn your back on waves
  • Cash: Bring cash for farmers markets and small vendors
  • Water: Always carry plenty of water, especially for hikes
  • Sunscreen: Use reef-safe sunscreen only - it's the law!

  • Kona to Mauna Kea: ~1.5 hours
